Bio

I am a passionate explorer by heart. Since childhood, I have been irresistibly drawn to the rich tapestry of historical art and culture that spans the globe. Through my extensive travels, I have been fortunate to visit renowned museums worldwide, gazing in awe at the celestial masterpieces adorning the Sistine Chapel, treading the ancient streets of Pompeii, and immersing myself in the treasures of the Louvre. I have delved into the depths as a diver, exploring the haunting remains of a World War II shipwreck off the shores of Bali. I have soared above barren deserts in a balloon, descending upon a Bedouin camp in Dubai. I have felt the raw power of the Trishuli River as I navigated its rapids while rafting in Nepal, and I have embraced the exhilaration and intensity of skydiving.

Each of these experiences has indelibly etched vivid images in my mind. It was during these moments that the seed of learning graphic design and motion graphics took root within me. My designs predominantly reflect the art I have witnessed and the diverse encounters I have encountered during my travels. I perceive myself as a visual artist. Though not a professional designer, I approach my work with wholehearted dedication and passion.
